[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the weather changing when you look my way
A quiet kind of thunder at the end of the day
Every shadow in the room is pulling me to you
Falling in the dark is all I wanna do

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers while the evening slows
I trace the little lines where your shoulder shows
We left the noise behind on a quiet street
Now all I wanna hear is your heartbeat

[Pre-Chorus]
There is a fever rising, but it is sweet and slow
A burning kind of secret only we could know
You are leaning a little closer, letting down your guard
And losing all my senses is not very hard

[Chorus]
So let it come down, a love like soft rain
Washing out the world and easing every pain
Just an ocean of you, pooling in the night
Holding onto me until the morning light

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Falling down, falling down)
Washing over me without a single sound

[Verse 2]
Your breath is like a whisper on my collarbone
Making me forget I ever was alone

You bring a steady rolling river to its knees

[Echo] (Falling down, falling down)

[Bridge]
Falling in the dark is all I wanna do
Everything I have wanted is standing here with you
Let the midnight take us, let the shadows bleed

The world outside is fading, fading to a blur
